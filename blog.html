entry 16/04

I am trying to understand Double-Dispatch, and here we go, this explanation is helping me to clarify the idea in my mind:

We could solve a binary operation using a functional programming approach, that is using a method with if and else statements to determine what's the data type of the argument passed in the method

But instead, we are using am OOP approach:

Instead of asking what kind of thing V is, we are calling a method on V

We are going to call a method on V
and have it to do the addition
telling what kind of thing we are, (and we know that!)
and this must be solved using a "self" keyword

This is the idea that I started to have in my mind to understand this, I will come back later when I have finished understanding how it works
